About Me

Hi there! My name is Chelsea and I am here to help you document the most precious moments in your family's life.

Whether it be newborn photos, or whole family portraits, I've got you covered.

I started this journey years ago when I became a mom. Money was tight, so instead of paying for expensive glamour shots, I set up my own photo shoots. My friends and neighbors loved my pictures so much they asked me to take pictures of their kids as well. While I can take the traditional portraits, my specialty is in setting up a magical scene for your little treasure.

When a session is scheduled, I like to make the time to speak with the parent at least a week beforehand to discuss what exactly we plan to accomplish. A few of our most popular shoots: Fairy Garden, Princesses, Unicorns, Cowboy Classic, Christmas, and Easter.

Your vision is my vision, so let's make some precious heirlooms today!
