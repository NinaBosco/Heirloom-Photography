What if my child hates posing?

Together we can determine what is neccessary to let your child have fun. Most of the time the littles don't even realize they are being photographed.

What should I bring?

When we have our pre-shoot phone conference we will discuss things like wardrobe and props. I have many of these things at the ready, but if you have somehting specific you are absolutely welcome to bring it yourself.

How flexible are you in scheduling?

As a mom, I understand exactly what it means to operate on "mom time" and we work accordingly. My goal is to make this process as stress free as possible.

What is this going to cost me?

All times include tear down and set up:

  • per hour = $100.00 (2 hour minimum, props not included)
  • per session = $500.00
  • per family = $350.00
  • events = $2000.00
  • travel expenses will be applied to destinations further than 10 miles from the studio

Can you photograph births?

If you are comfortable with me being there during arguably the most vulnerable moment of your life, then sure! I've got rubber boots.

When will my pictures be ready?

1-3 business days for prints, 5-7 business days for all other works (canvas prints, mugs, etc.)

Do you only do staged photographs or can you shoot an event?

I have photographed parties and holidays, as well as staged photos.

Do you do weddings?

No

Where do the photoshoots take place?

I do have a studio, but we can go wherever you'd like.

Does it cost more to shoot multiple children?

No, having more than one kid already costs you enough.
